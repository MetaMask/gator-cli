## [0.1.0-alpha.0]

### Added

- ERC-7710 delegation management - grant, redeem, revoke, and inspect delegations.
- Action based redeem flow with support for `erc20Transfer`, `erc721Transfer`, `nativeTransfer`, `functionCall`, `ownershipTransfer`, and `raw` modes.
- Native and ERC-20 token balance checks.
- Multi profile support with config and delegation storage.

### Changed

- Initial release
